❓ FAQ

How do I prevent mouse wrist pain?

Switch to a vertical mouse, use a trackball (eliminates arm movement), or ensure your mouse is at the same height as your keyboard. Take regular breaks and stretch.

Is 8K DPI necessary?

Not really. 800-2000 DPI is perfect for most monitors. Higher DPI is useful only if you have multiple high-res displays or need ultra-fast cursor movement.

Should I get a trackball?

If you have wrist or shoulder issues, yes. Trackballs let you move the cursor with your thumb instead of moving your entire arm — huge for long work sessions.
