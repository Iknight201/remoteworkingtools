❓ FAQ

Is 4K worth it for remote work?

Yes. 4K gives you twice the screen real estate of 1080p. Text is sharper, you can fit more windows side-by-side, and your eyes will thank you after 8 hours of work.

Do I need an ultrawide?

If you constantly have multiple windows open or hate dual monitor setups, ultrawides are amazing. They eliminate the bezel in the middle and give you one continuous workspace.

What about eye strain?

Look for monitors with blue light filters and flicker-free panels. Dell's ComfortView Plus and similar features reduce eye strain during long sessions.
