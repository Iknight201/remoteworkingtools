❓ FAQ

Electric vs Manual: Which is better?

Electric is worth it for most people. The ability to quickly adjust with one touch makes you more likely to actually switch positions. Manual desks work but require more effort to adjust.

What's the minimum desk height?

Most standing desks go as low as 24-26 inches. If you're under 5'4", look for desks with 22" minimum or consider a converter.

How do I prevent leg fatigue?

Start with 20-30 minutes of standing and gradually increase. Wear supportive shoes, use an anti-fatigue mat, and alternate positions throughout the day.
