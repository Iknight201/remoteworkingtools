❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are noise cancelling headphones worth it for remote work?

Absolutely. Good ANC can block out background noise like traffic, dogs, or household activity, helping you focus during deep work sessions. They also signal to others that you're in "work mode."

What's the best headphones for video calls?

The Bose QuietComfort Ultra and Sony WH-1000XM5 have the best microphones for calls. They use multiple mics and AI noise reduction to isolate your voice.

Should I get over-ear or on-ear?

Over-ear headphones (like all our recommendations) provide better passive noise isolation and are more comfortable for long sessions. On-ear can get uncomfortable after a few hours.
