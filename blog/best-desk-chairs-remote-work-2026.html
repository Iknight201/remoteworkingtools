❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a desk chair last?

A quality ergonomic chair should last 8-15 years with proper care. Budget chairs typically last 3-5 years. The Herman Miller Aeron and Steelcase Leap both come with 12-year warranties.

Is the Herman Miller Aeron worth the price?

For remote workers spending 8+ hours at a desk daily, yes. The cost-per-year works out to roughly $10/month for the Aeron — less than many coffee shop subscriptions. Your back will thank you.

What's the best chair for programmers/coders?

We recommend the Secretlab Titan Evo or Razer Iskur V2. Both offer excellent lumbar support for long coding sessions and have type-R or 4D armrests for keyboard/mouse positioning.

Do I need a headrest?

Not necessarily. Most ergonomic chairs work great without one. However, if you like to recline and relax during breaks, a magnetic headrest (like on the Newtral Hugger) is a nice add-on.
