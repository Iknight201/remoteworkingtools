❓ FAQ

Mechanical vs Membrane: Which is better?

Mechanical keyboards offer better tactile feedback and durability but are louder. Membrane keyboards are quieter and cheaper. For all-day typing, we prefer mechanical with tactile switches (like Browns or Reds).

Do I need a separate numpad?

Not necessarily. Most 75% or full-size keyboards include one. If desk space is tight, a compact keyboard is fine — you can use the number row or a wireless numpad.

What switches should I choose?

  • Browns — Tactile, good for typing
  • Reds — Linear, quiet, good for gaming
  • Blues — Clicky, very loud — not ideal for remote calls
