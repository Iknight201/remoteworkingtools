❓ FAQ

Is 4K webcam necessary?

Not for most people. 1080p is perfectly fine for video calls. 4K is great if you want the absolute best quality or plan to crop/zoom in post.

Do I need external lighting?

Good lighting makes ANY webcam look better. Even a simple ring light or window light makes a huge difference. All the webcams we recommend have good low-light correction, but more light = better video.

Should I use the built-in mic?

For casual calls, yes. For professional meetings, use a dedicated microphone or headset. Built-in mics pick up keyboard clicks and room noise.
